TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Welcome toSmart Books Books. These terms and conditions entail our complete course of action standards. Please go through the document carefully to get yourself acquainted with our procedure guidelines.

Domestic customers:

  • Once you confirm your order, it is presumed that you have read and agree to the terms and conditions stated in this document. Therefore, it is strongly recommended that you go through this document prior to confirming any order.
  • All prepaid orders are processed only after receiving full payment.
  • In case of COD orders, new customers will be contacted for address verification before processing any order. In case of failed verification, no orders will be processed.
  • To receive your COD orders successfully, please be present at your address with full payable amount. In case you are absent from the premises when our rider visits you, please inform us of the same immediately through our Facebook page or contact number.
  • Failure to do that will be considered No-Call, No-Show and the customer will be declared defaulter, and will not be entertained for COD in the future.
  • Any exception to this rule will first need to pay outstanding shipment charges before next order is processed.
  • Extreme care is taken to make sure that all prices are accurate at the time of publishing/customer interaction. However, in case any uncertain market element induces a price change, we are left with no option than to ask our customers a different price than the previously shared one.
  • In case of price increase, our customers can use any of the varied payment options to pay the difference in the price. In case of price reductions, the difference is adjusted in the next order the customer places with us.
  • A book will be considered damaged only if an error renders it incomplete to read. To return a damaged copy, send it to us within a week of receiving the book making sure it is not damaged further. Decision to replace the book will solely be upon Booksland’ discretion.
  • Subject to availability of books in our stock, orders are delivered to customers within 1-3 business days in case of prepaid orders. For COD/VPP orders, the delivery process may take up to 3-7 business days, subject to book(s) availability in our stock.
  • All investigation into delayed shipments will be carried out after the delivery TAT (Turn Around Time) is over.
  • All orders confirmed after 5 p.m. will be processed the next business day.
  • If any book in your order is out of stock, you will be informed before your order is sent to you.
